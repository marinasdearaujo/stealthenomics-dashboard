1. Fix the Webinar Funnel

Last month: 1,372 clicks → 8 registrations (5 qualified). Sub-1% conversion rate. We've moved from RingCentral to Zoom with our own domain. Now we need full tracking:

  • Meta pixel on confirmation page — enables switching from Traffic to Conversions objective
  • UTM parameters on every link — pre-built per channel in a shared spreadsheet (Facebook organic, Facebook ad, Chris LinkedIn, outbound DM, email — each gets a unique tagged URL)
  • Form data → HubSpot — one place to track registration to attendance to sale

2. Improve SMB Landing Page (Phase 1)

Every dollar we spend on Google Ads, webinar retargeting, and organic content is only as good as the page it lands on.

Optimization priorities:

  • Clear value prop above the fold — What is the Launch Box, who is it for, and why should they care?
  • Social proof — Chris's credibility (Forbes featured, COO story), client testimonials, specific results.
  • Stronger CTA — Clear next step. One primary action, not multiple competing options.
  • Qualifying form — Keep qualifying questions but reduce friction on non-essential fields.
  • Mobile optimization — Most ad traffic arrives on mobile. Fast load, clean conversion.
  • Pixel + tracking — Meta pixel and Google tag fire on form submission.

Timeline: LP improvements should be completed in Phase 1 (Days 1-3), before Google Ads launch. Sending paid traffic to an unoptimized page wastes budget.

Qualified Leads — Meta to Google

We tested two approaches on Meta for direct lead generation:

Instant forms → fake phone numbers, bot entries

Landing page conversion → low conversion rate

Conclusion: Meta's audience isn't in buying mode for direct lead gen. Move to Google Search where intent is real.

Google Ads Setup ($50/day)

  • Search campaigns: High-intent keywords — technology acquisition, managed IT, business launch consulting. 10-15 keywords, not broad match.
  • Lead form: Manual phone entry + qualifying questions (company size, timeline, challenge).
  • Display + Video: Retargeting only. No cold prospecting.
  • After 30 days: Compare contact rate vs Meta. Data-driven permanent allocation.

Free Diagnostic — Funnel Entry Point

Lower commitment than webinar (no date/time). Captures a lead, reveals gaps, creates a natural next step:

"Your score is 78%. Here are the 3 areas holding your business back. Want the full framework? Join our next live webinar — or join the community for ongoing support."

YouTube Strategy — Daily via Opus Clip + Showcases + Reach

  • Daily webinar cuts — Beverly uses Opus Clip (16-60s sweet spot). Share login with Beverly.
  • Product showcases — BPC demos, Launch Box walkthroughs. 1-2 per month.
  • Reach/growth content — Trend-based videos, hooks, timely topics to attract new viewers.

Boost the Winner Strategy

Post organically → after 24-48h, boost the best performer ($15/day, 3-5 days) → viewers enter retarget pool → retarget with webinar CTA. Cost: $0.01-0.10/view on boost vs $5-8/registration cold.

Facebook & Instagram — Repurpose Only

Facebook organic reach averages 15-27 per post. We're spending $135/day on Meta ads that reach thousands. FB and IG become repurpose channels: best LinkedIn posts and YouTube clips. No original creative time.

LinkedIn Outbound — Personal Invitations

For a high-ticket offer like the Launch Box, personal outreach from Chris can be more effective than any ad:

  • Target list: Founders, CEOs, owners of 1-50 employee companies. LinkedIn search filters by industry, size, geography.
  • Sequence: Personalized connection note (not a pitch) → share value/content → personal webinar invitation after engagement.
  • Volume: 10-20 connections/week. At 10% conversion = 4-8 registrations/month — hand-picked and free.
  • Compounding: Every connection stays in Chris's network for future webinars. Every post Chris makes reaches this growing audience.

Start Day 1. This doesn't depend on tracking being set up. Chris can start personally inviting founders to the April 21 webinar immediately.

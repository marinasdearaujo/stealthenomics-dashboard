Key Learnings

Working

  • LinkedIn company page: 17.9% ER — our strongest organic channel
  • HubSpot traffic: 92 → 772 (+739%) after strategy changes
  • Google Ads: Capturing real search intent at $50/day (Demond manages)
  • Beverly's content production: Consistent output across LI, YT, FB, IG, Community
  • Webinar conversion objective: Fixed from Traffic (1,372 clicks → 8 regs) to Conversions
  • YouTube Shorts: 39 subscribers from zero. 16-60s sweet spot performs best

Not Working / Blocked

  • Meta instant lead forms: Fake numbers, bot entries — CUT
  • Meta LP conversions: Sub-1% conversion rate — CUT
  • IG images: 2-8 reach per post — nearly invisible. Reels do better
  • FB organic reach: Avg 44/post. Ads handle visibility — organic is repurpose only
  • Chris's personal LinkedIn: Blocked by friction. 561% more reach untapped
  • Jerico out: Lost CRM, engagement, outbound, and email ops capacity
  • No email reminders for April webinar: Likely hurt show-up rate significantly

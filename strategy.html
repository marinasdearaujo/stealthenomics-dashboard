Key Learnings

Working

  • Webinar growth: 8 regs (March) → 34 regs (April) after switching to Conversions + UTMs. Hit target.
  • SMB list = gold: 9 regs → 8 attended (89% show-up). Warm list outperforms ads 3x on attendance.
  • Community growing: 8 → 19 members (+138%). Still needs critical mass but trending up.
  • Content engine: Beverly producing consistently. April was a transition month (new content calendar + reduced hours) — May has a well-drafted content strategy ready to execute

Not Working / Blocked

  • Meta lead gen: Both instant forms and LP conversions produced junk — CUT
  • No email reminders for April webinar: Ad registrants had only 28% show-up vs 89% from SMB list. Reminders would likely close this gap.
  • Chris's personal LinkedIn: Blocked by friction. Biggest untapped growth lever
  • Jerico out: Lost CRM, engagement, outbound, and email ops capacity
  • Community at 19 but low activity: Only 3 active/week, 0 comments. Growing but not engaging yet
  • Free Diagnostic: Live but underperforming — 48 views, 74% bounce, 0 new contacts. Page needs improvement before we drive traffic to it
